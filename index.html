01 · About

Curious about how systems work underneath.

I'm a second-year Information Science Engineering student with a growing obsession for what happens on the server side — the APIs, databases, and logic that quietly power everything a user touches on the surface.

My interest in backend development started with algorithms: implementing Prim's Algorithm for a network optimization project made me realize I enjoy the reasoning behind a system far more than just the interface. That curiosity has since pulled me toward Django, REST APIs, and databases — and toward genuinely understanding data structures instead of memorizing them for exams.

I learn best by building. Every project below started as "I wonder if I can make this work," and most of them didn't — until they did. I'm comfortable being a beginner in public, and I treat every bug as a small lesson in disguise.
